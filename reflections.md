## This will be my style cheat page 


### Emphasis
For _Italic_ use single " _ "

For **bold** use double " * "

### List
#### Unordered, you just use one " * "
* item 1
* item 2

#### Ordered, you just use one " 1. "
1. item 1
1. item 2
1. item 3
1. item 4
